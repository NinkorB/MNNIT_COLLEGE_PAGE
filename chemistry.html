About the Department

The department of Chemistry came into existence w.e.f. 1st April, 2003, prior to this it constituted a section of the Department of Applied Mathematics, Applied Sciences & Humanities.The department caters to the core engineering courses in Chemistry for B.Tech students in I/II Semester curriculum on branch basis. After attaining the status of National Institute of Technology, the department has undertaken research programme to Ph.D. degree. The department has floated six minor courses in Chemistry for Engineering Application for B.Tech. students from III to VI semesters and is to start M.Sc. in Applied Chemistry programme.

Undergraduate Programs (UG)

  • Advanced Polymer Materials
  • Chemistry of Nanomaterials
  • Organic & Physical Chemistry
  • Spectroscopic Methods of Analysis & Chromatographic Technique
  • Consumer Chemistry
  • Sustainability & Modern Chemical Technology
