Vision

To be a centre of excellence in Mechanical, Production, and Industrial Engineering education and research for the benefit of society and humanity.

Mission

  • To educate and develop competent human resources for contemporary industry and academia.
  • To promote interdisciplinary research and innovation skills in graduates.
  • To develop sustainable products, processes, and technologies by nurturing competent entrepreneurs.

About the Department

The Department of Mechanical Engineering, established in 1961, is one of the oldest departments in the institute. With 37 highly qualified faculty members, the department provides excellent education and research opportunities in three academic streams: Design Engineering, Production & Industrial Engineering, and Thermal Engineering.
