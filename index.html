Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology (MNNIT) Allahabad, Prayagraj, is a premier technical institute committed to academic excellence. Established in 1961 as one of Indiaâ€™s 17 Regional Engineering Colleges, it was initially affiliated with the University of Allahabad. It briefly affiliated with U.P. Technical University (2000-2002) before becoming an NIT and Deemed University in 2002. In 2007, it was granted the status of an Institute of National Importance.

MNNIT was the first in India to introduce a B.Tech. in Computer Science & Engineering (1976-77) and has since expanded to offer nine B.Tech., nineteen M.Tech., MCA, MBA, M.Sc., and MSW programs, along with Ph.D. research. The School of Management Studies was established in 1996. The institute actively supports faculty development, with most faculty members holding Ph.D. degrees.

MNNIT offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, science, and management. It is particularly known for its strong research culture, excellent faculty, and competitive student community. The institute also has a vibrant campus life with numerous technical and cultural fests, including Avishkar (tech fest) and Culrav (cultural fest).

MNNIT has been involved in various national and international projects, including the Indo-UK REC Project (1994-99) and TEQIP (2002-2007), reinforcing its role in quality technical education. The campus is fully networked with a 94 Mbps lease line and promotes entrepreneurship through an industrial estate with 68 sheds.
