Vision

The Computer Science & Engineering Department at MNNIT Allahabad aims to deliver leading programs in computing, producing globally recognized, innovative graduates. These graduates will excel as leaders in industry, education, and social life. The department will influence national and international agendas through quality teaching and research, while fostering interdisciplinary collaborations to enhance computing’s impact across fields.

Mission

  • Offer high-quality undergraduate, graduate programs, and technical skill training.
  • Employ qualified faculty committed to research and professional development.
  • Attract and retain a diverse, multicultural community of students, faculty, and staff.

About the Department

MNNIT Allahabad pioneered the first B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering in 1977, starting with 10 students under the Electrical Engineering department. Over time, it evolved into a separate department, gaining national and international recognition for excellence in computer science education and research. The department offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and Ph.D. programs, with expertise in areas like AI, ML, Data Science, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, and more. It collaborates closely with other departments and has developed several in-house software solutions, including portals for recruitment, academics, and crowd management used during the 2019 Kumbh Mela. The department is committed to continuous research and innovation aligned with the institute's mission and vision.
