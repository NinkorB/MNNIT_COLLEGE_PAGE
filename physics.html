Vision

To offer quality education to science and engineering graduates, empowering them to contribute to the creation of a sustainable future.

Mission

  • To provide fundamental physics knowledge as a foundation for engineering courses.
  • To prepare postgraduate students for challenges in advancing science and technology.

About the Department

The Department of Physics, MNNIT Allahabad came into existence from April, 2003. Prior to this, it constituted a section of the Department of Applied Mathematics, Applied Sciences & Humanities. The Department offers Physics courses to all branches of B.Tech. students in their first year and runs Doctoral Program. The Department has dedicated B. Tech. laboratories for conducting lab experiments. The department is also going to start a "M.Sc. in Physics" postgraduate program. The program is approved by the Board of Governors and is likely to take admission from the session 2024-25.
There are eight (08) experienced faculty members in the department actively involved in teaching and various experimental as well as theoretical researches. The Department holds a competitive, cooperative, and progressive research ambiance along with quality synthesis labs and thin film deposition instruments along with characterization facility unit.
